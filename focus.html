Our Focus Dry crops • Small-scale farmers

Dry crops are the small-scale farmer’s game.

Shunah is built around dry crops grown by small-scale farmers — resilient, storable, tradable, and rooted in fragmented farm supply. We organize it end-to-end: aggregation, quality assessment, and delivery.

Structured Aggregation
Organizing fragmented supply through hubs and repeatable processes.
Farm-Gate Quality
Fast, consistent grading using computer-aided vision.
Market Connectivity
Connecting verified supply to structured local and export demand.
Infrastructure Layer
Building satellite + IoT visibility across the crop flow.
